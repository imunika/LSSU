Terese Gagnon

Terese Gagnon is a PhD candidate at Syracuse University, where she is writing her dissertation in dialogue with Karen plants, and sensory politics in forced migration and exile. She is the co–editor of Moveable Gardens: Iteneraries and Sanctuaries of Memory forthcoming from the University of Arizona Press in May 2021.
